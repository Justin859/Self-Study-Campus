Frequently Asked Questions



Q: Can I purchase more than one course at a time?
A: Yes, just add all the courses that you want to purchase to your cart and proceed to checkout.


Q: How can I start a course?
A: Register or Login to SelfStudyCampus. Once you login, you can search for a course. Select the course you want to study and select Add to Cart.
Once you have purchased your courses navigate to the My Courses page and follow the simple step by step instructions on how to redeem your courses.
Q: What do I do if I forgot my password?
A: If you forgot your Self Study Campus account password navigate to the Login page and click forgot password and enter your Self Study Campus email address to request a password reset.
If you forgot your portal login password navigate to the My Courses page and click Reset Portal Password.
